The Best Movies According to Romeo

My top three Movies of all time:

Don't Be a Menace to South Central

This a classic from the Wayans brothers that satirized inner-city African-American movies.

Soul

This highly acclaimed movie from Pixar leaves its viewers questioning their purpose in life and the point of life itself.

Everything Everywhere All at Once

This absurdist comedy-drama explores themes of nihilism among the current generation through the exploration of the Asian American identity.
